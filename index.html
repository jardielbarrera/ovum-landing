How it works

Three problems. One platform.

Designed for one hand, offline, at 6 AM.

01

Protocol scheduler

Set up a synchronization protocol once. Ovum schedules every injection, flush, and preg check automatically across all your mares. Overdue alerts surface first, grouped by ranch — so you walk in knowing exactly what needs to happen.

02

Embryo grading

Log a flush in the field in under 30 seconds. Select the donor, record embryos recovered, note the sire, and add observations — before you leave the stall. No clipboard. No transcription at the end of the day.

03

Cryobank & inventory

Every donor and recipient across every ranch, at a glance. Know who's overdue, who flushes today, and pull up a client's frozen embryo count in seconds — not from a drawer, not from a spreadsheet.
