The problem

Repro vets run one of the most data-intensive practices in veterinary medicine — on paper.

Protocols live in notebooks

Step-by-step flush protocols tracked by hand. One missed injection can cost a full cycle. There's no system catching it.

Flush outcomes never get analyzed

Embryo grades, recipients, sire data — recorded on paper and never seen again. No way to spot what's working across mares.

Every ranch is a different binder

Switching between clients means juggling stacks of paper. Nothing is searchable. Nothing is shared. Records get lost.

How it works

From the barn to a complete record in under a minute.

Step 01

Assign the protocol

Pick the protocol — Ovsynch, 7-day CIDR, or custom — and Ovum generates every injection date automatically.

Auto-dates: GnRH Day 0, Lutalyse Day 7.5, CIDR pull — calculated from one start date
20–40 mares at once: staggered schedules across multiple ranches, all in one view
Shift any date and downstream steps recalculate automatically
Step 02

Log each step as it happens

Today's tasks surface at the top — overdue first. One tap opens a pre-filled form. Injection, ultrasound, flush, preg check. Done in seconds.

Works offline — all data saves locally in dead-zone barns, syncs when you're back on WiFi
IETS embryo grading in 2 taps — Stage + Quality linked instantly to donor, stallion, and flush date
Drug log auto-updated — one-tap injection entry keeps DEA records current
Step 03

Every record, searchable forever

Embryo grades, sire data, recipient mares, outcomes — all structured and searchable. Spot patterns across mares. No binder, no lost notes.

Cryobank ledger: every frozen embryo tracked by tank / canister / goblet / straw position
Preg checks auto-scheduled — Day 14 and Day 25 added to your calendar the moment a transfer is logged
Season analytics: flush success rates, pregnancy rates, and embryo inventory — per ranch
