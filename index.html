The problem

Repro vets run one of the most data-intensive practices in veterinary medicine — on paper.

📓

Protocols live in notebooks

Step-by-step flush protocols tracked by hand. One missed injection can cost a full cycle. There's no system catching it.

🧫

Flush outcomes never get analyzed

Embryo grades, recipients, sire data — recorded on paper and never seen again. No way to spot what's working across mares.

📂

Every ranch is a different binder

Switching between clients means juggling stacks of paper. Nothing is searchable. Nothing is shared. Records get lost.

How it works

From barn to phone in four steps.

1

Add the mare and assign a protocol

7-day CIDR, Ovsynch, or custom. The timeline builds itself.

2

Log each step as it happens

Injection, ultrasound, flush, preg check — one tap from the task list.

3

Track embryo outcomes in real time

Grade, disposition, recipient mare — all captured at flush day.

4

Search any record, any mare, any ranch

Instantly, from your phone. No binders. No digging.
